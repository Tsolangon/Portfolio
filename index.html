Who Am I?

Welcome to my corner of the web!

I'm Therese J. Solangon,an IT student hailing from the vibrant city of Cagayan de Oro, Philippines. I'm thrilled to invite you to explore my world and get to know me a little better. As a second-year student at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines, I'm deeply immersed in the dynamic world of information technology. From coding to networking, database management to cybersecurity, I'm constantly honing my skills and expanding my knowledge base to thrive in this ever-evolving field. But beyond the confines of computer screens and lines of code, there's another side of me that finds solace and exhilaration in the world of sports. Since childhood, sports have been my constant companion, igniting a fire within me that continues to burn brightly. Whether it's the thrill of competition, the camaraderie of teamwork, or the discipline of training, sports have shaped me in profound ways.

On this website, you'll discover more about my academic pursuits in IT, my love for sports, and everything in between. Join me as I navigate the complexities of technology, pursue my passions with unwavering determination, and embrace the endless possibilities that lie ahead.

Education

Education

COLLEGE SCHOOL

I am currently a second-year IT student at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines.

This prestigious institution has provided me with invaluable opportunities to expand my knowledge, develop new skills, and engage with diverse perspectives.

SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

I graduated from Liceo de Cagayan under the ABM strand, where I developed a strong foundation in business, finance, and management principles, shaping my academic and professional pursuits.

Graduating from Liceo de Cagayan was a significant milestone in my educational journey, marking the culmination of years of hard work, dedication, and growth.

JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

I graduated from Misamis Oriental General Comprehensive High School, where I was part of the Sports Class program from Grade 7 to 10, focusing on chess, which helped me refine my skills and deepen my passion for the game, significantly contributing to my overall growth and development.

Misamis Oriental General Comprehensive High School (MOGCHS) is known as the 'Home of the Champion,' known for its legacy of fostering outstanding athletes who excel in various sports disciplines and bring honor to the school through their remarkable achievements.

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

In elementary, I graduated from South City Central School (SCCS) after nine years—three in kindergarten and six in grade school, where daily walks with my father fostered cherished family moments, and achieving a three-peat chess championship from grades 4 to 6 marked a proud accomplishment in my elementary days.

Elementary days are special because they're simple, fun, and where we learn the basics, make friends, and create happy memories that stay with us forever.
